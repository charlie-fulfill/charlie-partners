Charlie is a **Shopify-native platform** for omnichannel retail brands. It does exactly and only what Shopify doesn't : order routing, omnichannel analytics, better ship-from-store in-store operations, without adding a new tool for any team.

<Check>
  Charlie does exactly the 30% that Shopify doesn't.
</Check>

## Four core capabilities.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Smart routing" icon="route">
    Every order is assigned to the best location automatically — based on stock, proximity, store capacity, customer segment, cart value, or any combination. Zero manual work.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Safety stock" icon="shield-halved">
    Charlie reserves buffer inventory per location, before it reaches any sales channel. Sellable quantity is always accurate, based on available native inventory, in real time.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Capacity management" icon="gauge-high">
    Set a daily order limit per location. When a store hits the limit, Charlie reroutes automatically. No burnt-out teams.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Revenue attribution" icon="chart-line">
    Every order fulfilled in-store is credited to that location. Store managers see their real contribution — the right incentive for the right team.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What it enables in practice.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ship from store" icon="store">
    Warehouse out of stock? Charlie routes to the most relevant store. Staff ships directly from Shopify POS — no second screen, no new app.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Click and collect" icon="bag-shopping">
    Customers buy online, Charlie reserves sellable stock and manages the pickup queue in POS. No overselling.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Endless aisle" icon="arrows-left-right">
    Product not in this store? Staff can order from another location for home delivery — without leaving POS.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="VIP routing" icon="star">
    High-value or VIP-tagged orders route to a flagship automatically so teams can add a personal touch.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Capacity management" icon="gauge">
    A store hits its daily limit? Charlie stops routing there and redirects automatically. Zero manual intervention.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Revenue attribution" icon="chart-bar">
    Every fulfillment in-store is credited to that location. Retail teams see their number, in Charlie.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
