## Referring a client.

The simplest way to start: introduce us by email. A 2-line message is enough — we'll take it from there.

**Who to contact:** [partnerships@usecharlie.ai](mailto:partnerships@usecharlie.ai)

Tell us:

- The client's name and Shopify setup
- The context (build, POS project, advisory)
- Whether you want to lead or have us lead

We'll confirm receipt and agree on the next step with you before reaching out.

## Becoming a formal partner.

If you want to set up a referral agreement, get access to partner materials, and be registered for revenue share, register your agency and we'll take it from there.

**Partner contact:** [partnerships@usecharlie.ai](mailto:partnerships@usecharlie.ai)

<Card title="Register as a partner" icon="handshake" horizontal href="/working-together/become-a-partner">
  Send us a few details and we'll set up your onboarding.

Onboarding is simple — a short call, a partner agreement, and you're live on Stripe for automatic payouts.

## Technical questions.

For scoping questions, product details, or anything client-specific — reach out directly. We'd rather answer a question early than have a deal fall through over a misunderstanding.
