## Ready to refer a client?

The simplest way to start: introduce us by email. A two-line message is enough — we'll take it from there.

**Who to contact:** [hello@usecharlie.ai](mailto:hello@usecharlie.ai)

Tell us:
- The client's name and Shopify setup
- The context (build, POS project, advisory)
- Whether you want to lead or have us lead

We'll confirm receipt and agree on the next step with you before reaching out.

## Want to become a formal partner?

If you want to set up a referral agreement, get access to partner materials, and be registered for revenue share, reach out to discuss partner onboarding.

**Partner contact:** [hello@usecharlie.ai](mailto:hello@usecharlie.ai)

Onboarding is simple — a short call, a partner agreement, and you're live on Stripe for automatic payouts.

## Have a technical question?

For scoping questions, product details, or anything client-specific — reach out directly. We'd rather answer a question early than have a deal fall through over a misunderstanding.
