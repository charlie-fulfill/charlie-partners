Charlie's design system is precise, restrained, and data-forward. 2 typefaces. 1 accent color. No decoration that doesn't earn its place.

Use these guidelines whenever you represent Charlie in proposals, decks, or client-facing materials.

## Logo.

The Charlie logo is an 18x18px filled square mark with an inner L-bracket — a registration mark that reads as precise and architectural. It sits alongside the wordmark "Charlie" in Inter Tight, weight 600, letter-spacing -0.02em.

**Usage rules.**
- Maintain clear space equal to the mark width on all sides.
- Never stretch, rotate, recolor, or add effects to the mark.
- Never separate the wordmark from the mark in partner materials.

## Color.

A cool blue-gray neutral ramp with a single signal accent. Status colors are intentionally desaturated so they do not compete with the UI.

### Neutrals.

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| `--ink` | `#0a0f1a` | Primary text, headings |
| `--ink-2` | `#1a2233` | Secondary text, body copy |
| `--ink-3` | `#4a5468` | Tertiary text, labels, captions |
| `--ink-4` | `#78819a` | Placeholder, muted metadata |
| `--line` | `#dde1ea` | Hairline borders, dividers |
| `--line-2` | `#c8cdd9` | Secondary borders |
| `--paper` | `#f4f5f8` | Page background, card fills |
| `--paper-2` | `#edeff4` | Inset backgrounds, secondary panels |
| `--white` | `#ffffff` | Base surface |

### Accent and status.

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| `--accent` | `#0a66ff` | Live indicators, active states, chart highlights |
| `--ok` | `#147a43` | Fulfilled, success states |
| `--warn` | `#b46200` | Pending, warning states |
| `--danger` | `#a82436` | Error, critical states |

**Rules.** The accent is used once per surface — for signal, not decoration. No gradients, no tints, no photography, no illustrations. Backgrounds are flat neutrals only.

## Typography.

2 typefaces. No exceptions.

### Inter Tight.

Used for everything humans read: headings, body copy, UI labels, buttons.

| Style | Size | Weight | Tracking |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display | clamp(48px, 7.5vw, 112px) | 500 | -0.035em |
| H1 | clamp(36px, 4.5vw, 60px) | 500 | -0.028em |
| H2 | clamp(26px, 2.6vw, 36px) | 500 | -0.02em |
| H3 | 20px | 500 | -0.01em |
| Body lead | 17px | 400 | 0 |
| Body | 15px | 400 | 0 |
| Body small | 13px | 400 | 0 |

### JetBrains Mono.

Used for system chrome: eyebrows, stat tags, order IDs, timestamps, data labels, any code.

| Style | Size | Tracking | Transform |
|---|---|---|---|
| Eyebrow | 11px | 0.08em | Uppercase |
| Data | 13px | 0.04em | As-is |

Tabular numbers globally via `font-feature-settings: "tnum"`. Numbers always align.

## Voice.

Technical, precise, confident. Charlie writes for operators and engineers as peers — not as support tickets.

**Do.**
- Claim a position in the first clause. "Route every order against the policy that wins."
- Use numerals always: `14.8M orders/month`, `120ms`, `$3,800/mo`.
- Pair a claim with its counter-claim. "Priced on GMV. Not per seat."
- Use domain vocabulary without glossing: GMV, 3PL, SKU, SLA, ASN, EDI.
- Sentence case for all headings. End every heading with a period.

**Don't.**
- Exclamation marks. Em-dashes for drama.
- Title Case in headings.
- Hedging: "maybe", "sort of", "flexible pricing tailored to your needs".
- Filler adjectives: "game-changing", "powerful", "innovative".
- Emoji or unicode decoration.

**Example lines.**
- "One ledger for every channel, every warehouse, every SKU."
- "Reverse logistics that don't bleed margin."
- "Numbers your CFO will sign off on."

## Motion.

3 easings. No parallax, no spring physics, no Lottie.

| Name | Easing | Duration | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Instant | `linear` | 120ms | Toggles, hover, chip selection |
| Entrance | `cubic-bezier(.2,.7,.2,1)` | 500ms | Scroll reveals, element mount |
| Transition | `cubic-bezier(.7,0,.3,1)` | 400ms | Page and panel changes |
