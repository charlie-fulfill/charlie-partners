## The company

Charlie is a Paris-based SaaS company building the omnichannel operations platform for Shopify Plus brands.

Founded on a simple observation: Shopify transformed retail commerce, but the tools brands need to operate across stores, warehouses, and online channels never caught up. Classic OMS platforms were built for a pre-Shopify world — expensive, slow to deploy, and designed for IT teams, not retail operators.

Charlie was built to close that gap, natively inside Shopify.
