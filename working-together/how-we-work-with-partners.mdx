Charlie is built to work alongside agencies, not around them. Charlie brings the product, your agency brings the relationship and the expertise.

## When Charlie is relevant for you.

3 moments where introducing Charlie creates real value for your agency and your clients.

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="On a Shopify build" icon="hammer">
    You're scoping or delivering a full Shopify project for a multi-location brand. Adding Charlie to the proposal strengthens your answer on unified commerce — without adding complexity to your delivery.
  </Card>
  <Card title="On a Shopify POS project" icon="shop">
    You're helping a client go live on Shopify POS. Charlie significantly increases your chances of closing and cuts time to market — a fast, low-cost OMS answer removes one of the last objections to adopting POS.

    Shopify POS is a major focus for both Shopify and agencies right now. Charlie makes it easier to sell.
  </Card>
  <Card title="In an ongoing advisory relationship" icon="arrows-rotate">
    You manage a client's Shopify environment on a retainer basis. Introducing Charlie is a low-effort way to bring strategic value to an existing account — without a new project.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Two levels of opportunity for your agency.

Beyond the referral fee, Charlie creates 2 additional commercial opportunities.

**1. Consulting on omnichannel and order routing.**

Understanding how order routing works, what fulfillment constraints apply, and how to configure Charlie for a specific brand requires expertise. That expertise can — and should — be sold by your agency.

Charlie does not sell consulting to brands. That's intentional. The advisory work is yours.

**2. Front-end development.**

Charlie's safety stock feature requires custom front-end development to surface real-time stock data in the right places. Depending on the client's exact needs, this represents a few days of billable dev work.

A dedicated page in the Technical section explains exactly where this development work sits and what it involves.

## How the collaboration works.

There is no fixed process. You choose the mode that works best for your client relationship.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Your agency in the lead" icon="user-tie">
    You run the client relationship end to end. Charlie supports you in the background — available for technical questions, demo support, or scoping — but stays out of sight with the client.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Charlie in the lead" icon="presentation-screen">
    Charlie takes the client-facing role — discovery, demo, closing. Your agency stays in the loop at every step and we report back to you throughout.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

In both cases: Charlie never sells consulting, never manages the client relationship without you, and never goes around your agency.
