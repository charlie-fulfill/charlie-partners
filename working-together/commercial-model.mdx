## What your clients pay.

Charlie is a monthly subscription billed based on the merchant's online GMV. No setup fees, no CAPEX. All plans include a 15-day free trial.

| Plan | Online monthly GMV | Monthly price | Support |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Basic | Up to \$200k | \$350/mo | Email |
| Starter | Up to \$1M | \$700/mo | Dedicated |
| Growth | Up to \$1.5M | \$1,750/mo | Dedicated \+ white-glove onboarding |
| Scale | Up to \$2.5M | \$3,000/mo | Dedicated \+ white-glove \+ customer success |

<Info>
  All charges are in USD, billed every 30 days. Contracts are signed directly between Charlie and the merchant.
</Info>

## What you earn as a partner.

### Referral fee. Recurring rev share.

A recurring rev share agreement is available to registered partners.

<Info>
  Rev share rates are shared as part of the partner onboarding process. To set up your agreement, reach out via the contact page.
</Info>

### Development fees.

Charlie's safety stock feature requires custom front-end development. Scoped and billed directly by your agency to the client — typically 2 to 5 days of work.
