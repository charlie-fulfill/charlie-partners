## What your clients pay.

Charlie is a monthly subscription billed based on the merchant's online GMV. No setup fees, no CAPEX. All plans include a 15-day free trial.

| Plan | Online monthly GMV | Monthly price | Support |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Basic | Up to \$200k | \$350/mo | Email |
| Starter | Up to \$1M | \$700/mo | Dedicated |
| Growth | Up to \$1.5M | \$1,750/mo | Dedicated \+ white-glove onboarding |
| Scale | Up to \$2.5M | \$3,000/mo | Dedicated \+ white-glove \+ customer success |

<Info>
  All charges are in USD, billed every 30 days. Contracts are signed directly between Charlie and the merchant.
</Info>

## What you earn as a partner.

### Referral fee. Recurring rev share.

For every client you refer, you receive a percentage of their monthly subscription for up to 2 years. The rate scales with the client's plan.

| Plan | Monthly price | Your rev share | Earnings |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Basic | \$350/mo | 5% | \$420 |
| Starter | \$700/mo | 7.5% | \$1260 |
| Growth | \$1,750/mo | 10% | \$4200 |
| Scale | \$3,000/mo | 12% | \$8640 |

### How payouts work.

Referral fees are paid automatically via Stripe every month. Nothing to invoice, nothing to chase — once your referral link is set up, payments land in your account automatically.

### Development fees.

Charlie's safety stock feature requires custom front-end development. Scoped and billed directly by your agency to the client — typically 2 to 5 days of work.

## The full revenue picture.

For a Growth client, the opportunity across year one:

| Revenue stream | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Referral fee (12 months) | \$2,520 |
| Onboarding consulting | 3-5 days advisory |
| Safety stock front-end dev | 2-4 days development |

<Tip>
  The referral fee is recurring. Every client you refer keeps earning — without any additional effort on your side.
</Tip>
