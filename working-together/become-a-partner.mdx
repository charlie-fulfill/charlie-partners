Charlie partners with Shopify agencies and consultants who advise multi-location brands. To register, send us an email with a few details — we'll reply within one business day and set up your onboarding.

## Register your agency.

<Card title="Send your registration email" icon="envelope" horizontal href="mailto:partnerships@usecharlie.ai?subject=Partner%20registration&body=Agency%20name%3A%20%0AWebsite%3A%20%0AYour%20name%20and%20role%3A%20%0ALocation(s)%3A%20%0ANumber%20of%20Shopify%20Plus%20%2F%20POS%20clients%3A%20%0ATypical%20engagement%20(build%20%2F%20POS%20%2F%20advisory)%3A%20%0AAnything%20else%20we%20should%20know%3A%20%0A">
  Opens a pre-filled email to [partnerships@usecharlie.ai](mailto:partnerships@usecharlie.ai). Fill in the blanks and hit send.

Include the following so we can prepare your onboarding:

- **Agency name** and website
- **Your name and role**
- **Location(s)** you operate in
- **Number of Shopify Plus / POS clients** you work with
- **Typical engagement:** build, POS project, or advisory
- Anything else we should know
