---

You don't need to know Charlie inside out to surface the right problems. These questions are designed to open up the conversation naturally — and help you quickly qualify whether Charlie is relevant for a given account.

## On order routing

> *"When an online order comes in, how do you decide which location fulfills it?"*

> *"Is that logic automated, or does someone make that call manually?"*

> *"Do you ever override Shopify's default assignment? How often?"*

> *"Have you had situations where the wrong location fulfilled an order — and it caused a problem?"*

## On inventory and stock accuracy

> *"Do you ever oversell online because a store hasn't updated its stock in time?"*

> *"Is there a buffer between what Shopify shows as available and what you actually want to sell?"*

> *"How do you manage safety stock across locations today?"*

## On store capacity

> *"During peak periods, do your stores ever get overwhelmed with online orders to fulfill?"*

> *"Is there a daily limit on how many orders each location can handle? How is that enforced?"*

> *"What happens when a store is at capacity — do orders pile up, get reassigned, or just cause problems?"*

## On revenue attribution

> *"When a store fulfills an online order, does that store get credit for the revenue?"*

> *"Do your store managers know how much they're contributing to online sales?"*

> *"Is store performance measured only on in-store sales, or does it include fulfillment contribution?"*

## On their current setup

> *"Are all your sales channels — online, stores, wholesale — running through Shopify?"*

> *"Have you looked at a standalone OMS before? What happened?"*

> *"What's the one fulfillment problem you'd fix tomorrow if you could?"*

## Reading the answers

| What you hear | What it signals |
|---|---|
| "We do it manually" | Smart routing pain |
| "We oversell sometimes" | Safety stock gap |
| "Stores get overwhelmed at peak" | Capacity management need |
| "Store managers don't see online contribution" | Revenue attribution gap |
| "We looked at an OMS but it was too heavy" | Charlie's sweet spot |
| "We have channels outside Shopify" | Potential fit issue — dig deeper |
