You don't need to know Charlie inside out to surface the right problems. These questions are designed to open up the conversation naturally — and help you quickly qualify whether Charlie is relevant for a given account.

## On order routing.

> _"When an online order comes in, how do you decide which location fulfills it?"_

> _"Is that logic automated, or does someone make that call manually?"_

> _"Do you ever override Shopify's default assignment? How often?"_

> _"How are online orders distributed across your locations today and does it feel balanced?"_

## On inventory and stock accuracy.

> _"Do you ever oversell online because a store hasn't updated its stock in time?"_

> _"Do you need to protect some products in store from being sold online ?"_

> _"Is there a buffer between what Shopify shows as available and what you actually want to sell?"_

> _"How do you manage safety stock across locations today?"_

## On store capacity.

> _"During peak periods, do your stores ever get overwhelmed with online orders to fulfill?"_

> _"Is there a daily limit on how many orders each location can handle? How is that enforced?"_

> _"What happens when a store is at capacity — do orders pile up, get reassigned, or just cause problems?"_

## On revenue attribution.

> _"When a store fulfills an online order, does that store get credit for the revenue?"_

> _"Do your store managers know how much they're contributing to online sales?"_

> _"Is store performance measured only on in-store sales, or does it include fulfillment contribution?"_

## On their current setup.

> _"Are all your sales channels — online, stores, wholesale — running through Shopify?"_

> _"Have you looked at a standalone OMS before? What happened?"_

> _"What's the 1 fulfillment problem you'd fix tomorrow if you could?"_

## Reading the answers.

| What you hear | What it signals |
| --- | --- |
| "We do it manually" | Smart routing pain |
| "We oversell sometimes" | Safety stock gap |
| "Stores get overwhelmed at peak" | Capacity management need |
| "Store managers don't see online contribution" | Revenue attribution gap |
| "We looked at an OMS but it was too heavy" | Charlie's sweet spot |
| "We have channels outside Shopify" | Potential fit issue — dig deeper |
